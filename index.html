Quick Start

  1. Deploy these files to Netlify/Vercel (see README_DEPLOY.md).
  2. Point rblotterytracking.com to your host.
  3. Add your AdSense IDs to ads-demo.html.
  4. Upload ads.txt to the root (we included a placeholder).
